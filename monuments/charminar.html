Char Minar, Hyderabad
Overview

The Charminar was built at the intersection of the historical trade route connecting the markets of Golkonda with the port city of Machhilipatnam.
The city of Hyderabad was designed with the Charminar at its center, spread around in four quadrants along the four cardinal directions. Mir Momin
Astarabadi of the Qutb Shahi dynasty played an important role and ordered extensive preparations for the design and layout along with that of th
new capital city. Architects from Persia were invited to provide additional designs and suggestions.
