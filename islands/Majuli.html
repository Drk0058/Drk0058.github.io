Attractions:  One should surely visit the twenty-one Satras which are present on the island. 
Some other places include Kamalabari Satra, Dakhinpat Satra, Garmur, Tengapania etc. 
Where To Stay: There are no hotels in Majuli. However, there are guest rooms present in the 
Satras. The Satras have facilities available for the tourists.
How To Reach: Jorhat is a place which is very well-connected by flights and is located at a 
distance of 14 km from Neemati Ghat.  Two ferry rides begin from here till Manjuli at 10 AM and 
3 PM respectively. 
 Best Time To Visit: The best time to visit the island is during the months of July to March on 
 the onset of monsoon as well as winter season.
